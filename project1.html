AI Project: DriveSmart

This was a group project at university where our AI solution aims to improve road safety by monitoring accident-prone areas and providing real-time alerts through a color-coded route system. Integrating GPS with historical data analysis, our system guides drivers safely, enhancing traffic flow. The report outlines the implementation, potential models, and ethical considerations of our AI solution. We aim to reduce accidents and promote sustainable development by refining the system based on user feedback and insights from a prototype.

Prototype Development

Data Collection

Online Databases: We integrated GPS technology to provide location-based services and real-time navigation features, enhancing user experience by offering accurate and timely information.

APIs: We explored APIs provided by transportation authorities or government agencies. These APIs offer access to real-time or historical accident data, streamlining data retrieval and providing structured datasets.

Data Scraping: We developed web scraping scripts to extract relevant data from online sources, adhering to legal and ethical guidelines. Data scraping allows for custom data extraction tailored to specific requirements.

Surveys: We conducted surveys and questionnaires to gather additional information, particularly concerning road conditions, weather, and witness accounts for specific incidents. Surveys provide context-rich data for in-depth analysis.

Technology Integration

GPS Integration: We utilized online databases from government websites and transportation departments, which often publish detailed accident reports, ensuring data reliability.

NLP: We developed a chatbot using NLP techniques to allow users to report accidents and hazards through natural language inputs. This facilitates easier and quicker reporting and improves data collection accuracy.

Machine learning: We utilized TensorFlow, PyTorch, and Scikit-learn to build predictive models. Techniques such as logistic regression, random forest, and SVM were employed to analyze historical accident data and predict accident hotspots.

UX/UI

Designed an intuitive and user-friendly interface featuring interactive maps, color-coded routes, and clear visual indicators for accident alerts and navigation suggestions using Pronto IO. This ensures users can easily interpret and interact with the app.
