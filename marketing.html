Digital Marketing in Real Estate: Increasing online traffic

In this project, a real estate company was struggling to generate leads and increase its online presence. Their website had very low traffic, with only a few active users visiting. They weren’t getting enough visibility or attracting potential customers.

Solution: I used analytics tools like Google Analytics and Google Search Console to understand and improve the website’s performance. This included conducting keyword research to identify terms potential buyers were searching for. I then optimized the website content by adding relevant keywords and highlighting key features that customers look for. I also focused on enhancing the user experience by making the website easier to navigate and more visually engaging. Additionally, I used crawling and indexing strategies to improve the site’s visibility in search engines

Outcome: Within a month, website traffic increased significantly with organic search, and the company began receiving more sales inquiries.
