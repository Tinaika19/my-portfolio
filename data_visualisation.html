Data Visualisation

This project focuses on generated visualizations and dashboards and comes up with recommendations. Over the years, the pharmaceutical, personal care, and nutritional industries have converged, leading to the emergence of new industry segments: Nutraceuticals, Cosmeceuticals, and Nutricosmetics. However, when examining the demand for these segments in Australia, notable differences become apparent. Consumers have varying preferences and make distinct choices when purchasing these products, making it essential to observe changes in consumer behavior. To promote sales effectively, we need to focus on products that may not be as popular among consumers, specifically Cosmeceuticals, Nutricosmetics, and Nutraceuticals

The overall insights that are gathered from the two analytical dashboards generated in PowerBI and SAP Lumira are that the demand of cosmeceuticals is considerably low compared to Nutricosmetics and Nutraceuticals in New South Wales and Western Australia. Also, social media is the most popular channel among people of various age groups. Therefore, it is highly recommended that companies should have online presence to boost sales

Dashboard using PowerBI

With regards to the above dashboard generated in PowerBI, if is quite evident that majority of the people living in NSW are ready to purchase nutraceuticals. While there is demand in cosmeceuticals in Queensland. Moreover, majority of the people are confident in the results of nutricosmetics products. Finally, most of the people prefer to consume pills for nutricosmetic products and least being food and beverage in Cosmeceuticals

Dashboard using SAP Lumira

With reference to the above dashboard generated in SAP Lumira, we can observe that age groups less than 30 years are confident in the results of nutricosmetics products. While people aged 40- 50 years are confident in cosmeceuticals. Moreover, we can also see that people are ready to send on nutricosmetics products among males and females. On the other hand, social media is quite popular among various age groups. Finally, the majority of people earn around 70,000-90,000 dollars in Victoria.

Suggested recommendations

Social media

It is crucial for any business to have an online presence, and social media is a popular channel across various age groups for contacting people. Businesses should leverage digital marketing on platforms like Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram to advertise and attract more customers. Facebook, with over 2.9 billion users, offers many features for selling, such as Facebook Pages, Messenger, Instagram, and WhatsApp. Businesses can also create a Facebook Shop section to sell products and display information like prices and offers to persuade consumers to make purchases

Packaging and pricing

Focusing on packaging and pricing on products are crucial. A study has shown that more than 50% of customers check the quality of the supplement with the product information and container label. Companies can also use attractive packaging and providing health benefits for each 9 Cosmeceuticals, Nutricosmetics and Nutraceuticals products. For example, prices of Nutraceuticals products can be substantially moderate to high in New South Wales since people are willing to spend on them.

Customer retention

It is important to attract new customers and not only improving customer retention. Promoting cosmeceutical products in Queensland and NSW can be done by giving perks to regular customer like discounts and free samples when launching a new product.
