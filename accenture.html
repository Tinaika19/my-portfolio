Accenture AI Project: Access Point

This AI project was part of the Accenture Women in Technology Bootcamp, an intensive four-day, hands-on program. We collaborated in groups to design a solution for a given problem and presented our work in front of judges. Our project involved creating an interactive app using AI to assist employees with disabilities in accessing information about their workplace's inclusion policies and disability guidelines. This experience provided me with valuable real-world experience, allowing me to work effectively in a team while honing both my technical and soft skills.

About Access Point

We developed an interactive app called AccessPoint using AI to help employees with disabilities access information about their workplace's inclusion policies and disability guidelines. Some of its features includes:

  • As a user with physical impairments, they can easily find out what assistance my company offers for disabilities so that they can be aware and prepared to begin work.
  • Users can select their specific disability needs and request assistance across multiple disability spectrums.
  • Users can contact their employer with any request they need.
  • Users are provided with their employerâ€™s email details upon request.

UX/UI Design

We aimed to design a user interface that is both simple and intuitive, ensuring that people with disabilities can navigate the app effortlessly. Additionally, we chose a light color scheme to enhance accessibility and avoid bright, overwhelming colors.

Solution Architecture

This is our solution architecture that describes the flow of operation as well as some of the front end and backend technologies used The user can interact with our chatbot to request information about workplace accommodations for disabilities. For example, the user can send a message by interacting with the frontend, where we have used HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. The chatbot will then provide information back to the user. On the backend, we used Node.js and JavaScript to handle these requests. Lastly For storing information, we used a software called Pinecone. Therefore, we have created a seamless and user-friendly experience for our users
