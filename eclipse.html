Eclipse

Eclipse was founded in 2018 as a university project that I worked on as part of a team, where we were responsible for designing and creating a wireframe focused on UX/UI. The project revolves around Eclipse, a makeup brand that offers a wide range of products tailored to meet diverse beauty needs.

Website Features

Video demostration

Our customers will have the opportunity not only to watch step-by-step tutorial videos on makeup application but also to explore detailed information about the products used in each look. Each video will include links to the featured products, allowing customers to easily browse through product descriptions, benefits, and reviews. This seamless integration ensures that customers can make informed purchasing decisions and recreate their favorite looks with confidence. Additionally, they can access personalized recommendations based on their preferences and past purchases, enhancing their overall shopping experience.

Social Media

Customers can showcase their experiences by posting images featuring our products on social media platforms. By using our dedicated hashtags, they can share their looks with a wider audience, connect with like-minded individuals, and become part of our growing community. This also provides them with an opportunity to inspire others and gain visibility through potential features on our official social media channels.

Chatbot

In case of any queries, customers can rely on our AI-powered chatbot for instant assistance. Whether they need help choosing the right products, understanding application techniques, or tracking their orders, the chatbot is available 24/7 to provide quick and accurate responses.
